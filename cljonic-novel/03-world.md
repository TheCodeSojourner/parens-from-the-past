## Key Places (GPS-accurate, walkable)

1. **Alte Stellmacherei**  
   Kirchstraße 12, 64625 Bensheim  
   Real 200-year-old wagon workshop → Thursday-night hacker space  
   Wooden beams, 3-phase power, one ancient couch that smells of Club-Mate

2. **Amalienstraße 17 barn**  
   Former winery storage → Cljonic HQ  
   12 m × 8 m concrete floor, roll-up door, skylight that leaks when it rains sideways  
   Exact coordinates: 49.6808° N, 8.6195° E

3. **Auerbach glider field**  
   49.6950° N, 8.6280° E  
   Abandoned 1930s Luftwaffe training strip → 256-drone lambda sky  
   7-minute bike ride from the barn, surrounded by cherry orchards

4. **Bensheim Hauptbahnhof**  
   Platform 3, north end → where Kai Le arrives from Frankfurt at 22:14

5. **Mathematikum Gießen**  
   Liebigstraße 24, 35390 Gießen  
   30-minute train ride → bronze lambda sculpture, exact spot of Chapter 1
