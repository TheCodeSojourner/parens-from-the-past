# 04-THEME-AGEISM-ARC.md

## The Ageism Arc – “Obsolescence is a Lie”

This is the **slow-burn gut-punch** that runs under every chapter like a 60 Hz hum.  
It is never announced.  
It is only felt.

## NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
1. The word “old” is **never** used as an insult.  
   It is only ever used as a unit of persistence.
2. Alex **never** complains about ageism.  
   Everyone else does it for him — and then spends 12 chapters realizing they were wrong.
3. The arc ends with the youngest person in the room defending the oldest with tears in her eyes.
